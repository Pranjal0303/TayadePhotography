About Us

About Us

"We change the lens to change the story"

We would love for our work to be seen as a celebration of human endeavour, whether concentrating on an individual’s personal sporting pursuits, or the artistry and craft involved in a highly skilled, labour-intensive industry. We shoot in a fast, fluid fashion, creating situations that build believable scenes, whilst encouraging and allowing our subjects the freedom to move, laugh, enjoy and be themselves. Much of our photography incorporates people who don’t model professionally, so we allow plenty of opportunities to converse before and during the shoot, putting them at ease in front of the camera. Our inclusive and collaborative approach to work produces fun, genuine moments as they unfold. It keeps shoots spontaneous, light and full of energy, and to the best of our ability, will give a very natural, emotive final image
